Santa Elena Project Site Planning

Site Name

Site Name: Santa Elena Wine and Liquor Winery

Description: This name represents the activities carried out in the company. Tradition, quality, and the essence of the winemaking process.

Optional Domain Availability: bodegasanta.pe

Site Purpose

Purpose: For tourists visiting the Tacna city, specially Chileans

To promote and sell the wines and liquors of Santa Elena Bodega, as well as build a community around the brand.

Scenarios

Scenario 1

Question: A potential customer living outside the region wants to try Santa Bodega wines. How can they view the catalog and purchase online?

Scenario 2

Question: A local resident is looking for a specific wine to pair with a special dinner. How can they find recommendations and tasting notes on the site?
